Beyond the Podium:

A Century of Performance and Progress



The Olympics. One of the most famous events in the world, whose name recalls years of practice and weighty cultural significance dates back to 776 BC, where they were help in Athens, Greece, almost 3000 years ago.



The modern Olympic Games as we know them, however, were first held in 1896, though still in Athens, and brought together 14 nations. Although at the time, a far cry from the behemoth we recognize the Games as today, they have increased exponentially in size, participation, and relevance.

The Olympic Games have increasingly become an international event.
